# Source priority reference

Use this order when available:
1. Official website pages
2. Official requisites / legal / privacy / contract pages
3. Official PDFs, brochures, presentations, exhibitor profiles
4. Official event speaker pages
5. Public procurement / filings / registry documents
6. Reputable business registries
7. Public professional profiles with clear company-role match
8. Official corporate social profiles

Avoid low-trust aggregators when better sources exist.
