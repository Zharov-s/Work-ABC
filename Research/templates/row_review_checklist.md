# Row review checklist

- Named person is the highest-priority publicly evidenced role
- Tenant-fit label is `PASS_STRONG` or justified `PASS_CONDITIONAL`
- Company is not warehouse-only, fulfilment-only, heavy production, pure office/software/retail, or cheap-workshop demand
- Premium-rent plausibility is evidenced
- No generic mailbox in Corporate email
- No general office number without extension in Work phone + extension
- Accelerated rows have both verified direct LPR email and verified mobile / named extension
- Source URL is strong and relevant
- Verification note explains blanks if needed
- Evidence log has supporting records
