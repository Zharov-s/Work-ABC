  service, commercial and mixed business formats.
- Address: Moscow, Baryshiha 37a.
- Location logic: Mitino, between Pyatnitskoe and Volokolamskoe highways; about
  15 minutes to Pyatnitskoe Shosse and Mitino metro stations; about 4 km to MKAD;
  convenient for staff, client visits, service trips, suppliers and daily logistics.
- Total area: about 11,776.20 m2.
- Production area: about 6,662.08 m2.
- Office area: about 3,400.93 m2.
- Power: 1.5 MW.
- Parking: about 70 parking spaces.
- Loading: 6 gates, including 3 with dock levellers; floor at 0.9 m for loading.
- Vertical logistics: 2 lifts and 2 freight lifts up to 4 t.
- Technical parameters: ceiling height up to 8 m; floor load up to 5 t/m2; column

- Commissioning: planned for Q3 2026.
- Lease structure: long-term lease; tenant improvements by prior agreement.
- Operating expenses: open book, capped at about 3,000 RUB/m2/year before VAT,
  utilities by consumption.
- VAT: public site currently states VAT 22% above the stated rates.
- Production rent: about 18,000 RUB/m2/year before VAT.
- Office rent: from about 28,000 RUB/m2/year before VAT.
- Commercial first-floor blocks: about 29,000 RUB/m2/year before VAT.
- Anchor proof point: Rubytech selected the site for production expansion of PAK
  and leased about 4,200 m2.
- Project operator/developer context: AB Centrum / ООО "ПРОМЦЕНТРПЛАСТ" is named
  on the project site; AB Centrum is described as a developer and general contractor
  of commercial and industrial properties in Moscow and Moscow Region.

## Core sales logic
This is not a cheap workshop or a warehouse. Treat it as an urban
production-client headquarters for a technology or premium physical-product company
that wants to combine production, engineering, team, clients, service and showroom in
one controlled Moscow environment.

## Practical implications for target selection
- The company should benefit from a polished Moscow address and client-facing
  environment, not only square meters.
- The use case should plausibly combine at least two of these functions:
  production, R&D, lab/testing, clean assembly, service center, showroom,
  engineering back office, customer demo, training, or technical support.
- The company must be able to absorb premium rent and fit Shell & Core delivery.
- Companies needing only low-cost production or bulk storage should be rejected fast.
- The best prospects are not necessarily the largest factories; they are companies
  where a Moscow mixed industrial-office site changes sales, operations, hiring,
  service quality, or brand perception.
