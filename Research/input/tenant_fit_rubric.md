# Tenant-fit rubric for AI agents

Use this rubric before investing time in LPR contact research. A company with great
contacts but weak fit is not a valid target for this project.

## Fast gate
Reject a candidate unless all three are true:
1. The operating profile fits VRI `6.3` light industry and/or VRI `6.12`
   scientific-production activity.
2. The company has a credible Moscow mixed-use need: production + office, lab +
   engineering, showroom + service, clean assembly + client demo, or similar.
3. The company looks commercially able to pay class A+ urban promtechnopark economics.

## Priority segments
Score these as strong fit when public evidence confirms the operating model:
- Electronics, electronic assembly, PCB/electronic modules, embedded hardware.
- Instrumentation, industrial automation, control systems, sensors, measurement.
- Server, telecom, network and data-center equipment; PAK and infrastructure
  hardware.
- Medtech, diagnostics, laboratory equipment, medical devices and clean assembly.
- Robotics, mechatronics, drones, autonomous systems and smart hardware.
- Photonics, optics, laser systems, microelectronics and precision devices.
- Engineering companies with hardware, prototypes, pilot production, testing or
  service operations, not pure consulting.
- Federal-brand service centers and technical-service companies that need a premium
  Moscow base with workshop, warehouse-light, office and client reception.
- Fashion, beauty and consumer-goods brands only when they need showroom + office +
  light assembly, samples, customization, repair/service, packaging or quality
  control. Pure retail does not qualify.
- Companies for which staff accessibility, metro, client visits, image and Moscow
  location are visibly important.

## Conditional segments
Research only if the public evidence shows a physical-product or technical-service
logic:
- Software or IT groups with their own hardware, PAK, devices, labs or service.
- Distributors or integrators with assembly, testing, demo rooms, service center or
  technical support, not only resale.
- Beauty/fashion/consumer brands with production, samples, repairs, lab, showroom or
  fulfillment-light workflows.
- R&D companies without visible revenue if they have funding, grants, corporate
  backing, contracts, resident status or meaningful headcount.

## Fast rejects
Do not spend contact-research time on:
- Classical warehouse tenants.
- E-commerce fulfilment and marketplace operators.
- Pallet storage, 3PL, dark stores and logistics-only businesses.
- Heavy, dirty, noisy, hazardous or high-utility production.
- Commodity workshops that need only the cheapest production area.
- Pure office, pure consulting, pure software, pure retail, horeca or education with
  no physical-product / lab / service / light-production footprint.
- Very small or obviously price-sensitive tenants unless there is strong evidence of
  funding, group backing, contract scale or a premium client-facing requirement.

## Affordability evidence
Prefer candidates with at least one strong signal:
- Revenue, profit, funding, group backing, public investment or M&A.
- Federal, enterprise, defense-adjacent, medical, telecom, industrial, export or
  government contracts.
- Manufacturing footprint, multi-site operations, notable headcount or job postings.
- Existing technopark, business park, industrial park, R&D campus or premium office
  occupancy.
- Public plans for expansion, localization, import substitution, service coverage or
  Moscow production.

## Fit labels for notes
Use one of these labels in every research note:
- `PASS_STRONG`: clear segment fit and clear premium-rent plausibility.
- `PASS_CONDITIONAL`: fit is plausible but needs a short explanation.
- `REJECT_SEGMENT`: outside target segment.
- `REJECT_ECONOMICS`: segment may fit but premium-rent plausibility is weak.
- `REJECT_CONTACT`: fit is good, but accelerated mode lacks required email + phone.
- `HOLD`: unresolved ambiguity; do not package until resolved.

## Agent rule
In accelerated mode, a company counts only after both gates pass:
- tenant fit gate is `PASS_STRONG` or a well-justified `PASS_CONDITIONAL`;
- contact gate has a verified direct person-level LPR email plus a verified direct
  mobile or named phone extension tied to the same LPR.
